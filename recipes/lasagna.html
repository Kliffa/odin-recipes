Description

Try this delicious recipe now!

This should be an easy to follow recipe, which I did not create myself, to prepare this amazing lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • Enough tomato products like cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 Onion (chopped into small pieces)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (crushed or finely chopped)
  • Italian herbs and spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • All the cheese you want

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven on 220 degrees celcius
  2. Fry the sausage and ground beef
  3. Add the onion and garlic
  4. Add tomato products
  5. Add the herbs & spices
  6. Layer the sause with the lasagna noodles
  7. Finish with a layer of all the cheese you want
  8. Bake the lasagna in your oven for 45 minutes
